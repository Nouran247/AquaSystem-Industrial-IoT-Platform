🌿 Watercress Conditions
Watercress thrives in cool, clean, oxygen-rich water with an optimal pH range of 6.5–7.5. The current system pH of 6.8 is ideal for healthy growth. The current temperature of 24.3°C is at the upper end of the recommended range (15–24°C). Continue monitoring water temperature during hot weather to maintain vigorous leaf production and prevent heat stress.
🐠 Goldfish Conditions
Goldfish perform best in water with a pH between 6.5–8.0. The current pH of 6.8 is well within the optimal range. The current water temperature of 24.3°C is suitable but close to the upper recommended limit (18–24°C). Ensure adequate aeration and maintain low ammonia and nitrite levels to promote fish health and support a stable aquaponic ecosystem.

About Us
Automated Aquaponic System
Smart Automated Aquaponics System is an IoT-based sustainable farming platform developed as a graduation project at Minia University. The system combines aquaculture (fish farming) and hydroponics (soilless plant cultivation) into a single, self-sustaining ecosystem where nutrient-rich fish waste is biologically converted into essential nutrients for plants, while the plants naturally filter and purify the water before it is recirculated back to the fish tank. By integrating advanced IoT technologies, environmental sensors, real-time monitoring, and intelligent automation, the platform continuously monitors and controls critical parameters such as water temperature, pH, water level, turbidity, total dissolved solids (TDS), and environmental conditions to maintain optimal growth for both fish and plants. Through a web-based dashboard, users can remotely monitor system performance, visualize live sensor data, receive alerts, and manage automated components, creating a resource-efficient, environmentally friendly, and scalable solution for modern sustainable agriculture.

🎯 Project Objectives
🤖
Automation
Automate dosing, valve control, and pump operations based on real-time sensor data without manual intervention.
🌿
Sustainability
Create a closed-loop ecosystem that minimizes waste and maximizes resource efficiency through aquaponics.
💧
Water Conservation
Recirculate water between fish and plant sections, reducing consumption by up to 90% versus traditional farming.
🌾
Smart Agriculture
Apply precision agriculture techniques using sensors, algorithms, and automated control for optimal yield.
📡
Remote Monitoring
Monitor and control the entire system remotely from any device through the web dashboard in real-time.
📊
Data Analytics
Collect, store, and analyze historical sensor data to identify trends and optimize system performance.
